How to Create an address block in Sheets from your Gmail or Outlook contacts using AddressBlockApp

Welcome to our tutorial on harnessing the power of AddressBlock to streamline your business management using spreadsheets. Follow these steps to make your workflow more efficient:

Adding Contacts

If you use other spreadsheets (Excel Worksheets, Sheets ect.) you can easily add them to your file:

  • Export your selected contacts to an Outlook cvs file. Open it and 'Select all' (ctl+a) then press (ctl+C) to copy. On the contacts sheet in the AddressBlockApp and paste them in the green shaded cell (B1).
  • Select the 'Name' dropdown box on the Address sheet. Copy and paste it in the desired cell on your form or spreadsheet.
  • Copy each formula of each cell of the address block onthe Address sheet and paste them into the formula bar of the desired cells on your form or spreadsheet.
  • Edit each formula by changing all B1 cell referances to the cell that you placed the dropdown box.

Congratulations! You've successfully integrated your contacts into your management workflow using the AddressBlock App. Enjoy the benefits of a more organized and efficient management experience.
