How to Create an address block in Google Sheets™ from your Gmail™ contacts using AddressBlockApp

Welcome to our tutorial on harnessing the power of AddressBlock to streamline your business management using spreadsheets. Follow these steps to make your workflow more efficient:

Adding Contacts

Open the AddressBlock menu and select Lookup by Name.

  • Export your selected Gmail™ contacts to an Outlook™ CSV file. Open it and 'Select all' (Ctrl+A) then press (Ctrl+C) to copy. Paste them (Ctl+V) in the green shaded (First Name) cell (B1) on the contacts sheet in the AddressBlockApp.
  • Select the 'Name' dropdown box (B1) on the Address sheet. Copy and paste it in the desired cell on your form or spreadsheet. (note the cell letter and number)
  • Select the Target cell on the Address sheet and enter the noted letter and number of your target cell. Select 'Target formulas by Name' in the menu.
  • When complete, select each formula in the formula bar press (Ctl+A) to select the formula then (Ctl+C) to copy. Select the desired cell on your form or speedsheet where you want the information to appear. In the formula bar, press (Ctl+A) then (Ctl+V) to paste, press Enter. Repeat this step for the rest of your address block.
  • Open the AddressBlock menu and select Lookup by Company. Export your selected contacts to an Outlook™ CSV file. Open it and 'Select all' (Ctrl+A) then press (Ctrl+C) to copy. Paste them (Ctl+V) in the green shaded (First Name) cell (C1) on the contacts1 sheet in the AddressBlockApp. Use previous steps to create your Address Block.

    • Congratulations! You've successfully integrated your contacts into your management workflow using the AddressBlock App. Enjoy the benefits of a more organized and efficient management experience.
