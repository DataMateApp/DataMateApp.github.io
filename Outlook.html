How to Paste Outlook Contacts into Your Project

Step 1: Open Outlook Desktop

Launch your Outlook Desktop application on your computer.

Step 2: Access People View

Click on the "People View" icon located in the lower-left corner of the Outlook window.

Step 3: Change View

In the "View" ribbon at the top of the screen, click "Change View" and select "Manage Views."

Select "List" and click "Modify..." to adjust the columns.

Customize Columns

Click "Columns..." and select "Address Fields" to add the following fields in the order shown:

  • Business Address Street
  • Business Address City
  • Business Address State
  • Business Address Postal Code

Ensure the fields appear in the following sequence for optimal viewing:

  • Icon
  • Attachment
  • Flag Status
  • Full Name
  • Job Title
  • Company
  • File As
  • Country/Region
  • Department
  • Business Phone
  • Business Fax
  • Business Address Street
  • Business Address City
  • Business Address State
  • Business Address Postal Code
  • Home Phone
  • Mobile Phone
  • Email
  • Categories
  • Assistant Phone

Apply the changes, select your contacts, then press "Ctrl+C" to copy them.

Step 4: Switch to the Contacts Sheet

Open the "Contacts" sheet. Find the cell labeled "Paste Outlook Contacts Here."

Step 5: Paste Contacts

Click on the cell and press "Ctrl + V" to paste the copied Outlook contacts into the sheet.

Step 6: Fill in Missing Information

Review the pasted contacts and fill in any missing information, such as project-specific details or additional contact information.

Congratulations! You've successfully pasted your Outlook contacts, enhancing your project management process.
