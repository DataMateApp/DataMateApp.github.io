The Story Behind DataMate

Construction Career

Years spent managing projects where spreadsheets were the backbone of operations.

The Idea

What if spreadsheets could act like lightweight databases without forcing users to learn complex software?

DataMate Was Born

A platform designed to bridge the gap between spreadsheets and traditional databases.

Why DataMateApps Are Free

Hi, I'm Dan Northway, founder and developer of DataMateApps. Before retiring, I spent my career managing construction projects where spreadsheets were used for nearly everything.

"What if I could turn spreadsheets into lightweight databases?"

That idea eventually became DataMate. The goal was never to replace enterprise systems or SQL databases. Instead, it was to give small businesses and teams a practical, affordable way to organize and manage information.

Today, DataMate remains free because the mission matters more than the software itself. Every user helps shape its future.
