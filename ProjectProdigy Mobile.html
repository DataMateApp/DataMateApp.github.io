📌 About ProjectProdigy & Download Requirements
ProjectProdigy is a free, lifetime construction project management system built entirely on Google Sheets™ and Google Drive™ — no subscriptions, no lock‑in.

It organizes, links, and synchronizes all project spreadsheets inside a single Drive folder, turning scattered files into a connected project workspace. Powered by DataMate, it supports estimating, contacts, RFIs, submittals, logs, dashboards, and CSI‑organized estimates.

Important: Installation requires Google Drive OAuth and must be completed on a computer or tablet. Mobile devices are supported for viewing only.
