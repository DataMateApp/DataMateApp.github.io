What DataMate Can Do for You

contacts

Contact Management

Easily manage contacts within Google Sheets™ and Excel™—no external CRM needed. Import from Gmail or CSV and keep everything in one place.

storage

Updatable Datasets

Store all your data in a single, structured sheet. Update, filter, and sort records like customer data or project logs with ease.

input

Streamlined Data Entry

Consolidate inputs into one system. Each entry becomes a record, seamlessly integrated into your dataset—no more scattered files.

history

Dynamic Log Tracking

Monitor changes with a log table supporting up to 12 fields per record. Stay organized and find entries instantly.

print

View & Print Records

Select, view, and print any record in a clean format—ideal for reports, audits, or presentations.

sort

Effortless Filtering

Instantly filter and sort records to analyze trends, update data, or compile reports—all within your spreadsheet.

Supercharge with FormBuilder

Create custom forms with 29 field types, including checkout tables for orders, hyperlinks for quick navigation, media-rich tables (images/videos), conditional logic, file uploads, and email notifications on submission. From surveys to e-commerce order forms, FormBuilder integrates seamlessly with DataMate, enhancing data collection in Google Sheets™.
