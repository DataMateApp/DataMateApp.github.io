AddressBlock for Google Sheets™

AddressBlockApp – Revolutionize Your Contact Management in Spreadsheets!

🌐 Seamless Integration
Easily create an address block in Google Sheets™ from your Gmail™ contacts with AddressBlockApp. Effortlessly import and organize your contacts!

📊 Streamlined Workflow Tutorial
Unlock the full potential of AddressBlock with our user-friendly tutorial. Follow these simple steps to enhance your business management:

  1. Adding Contacts
    • Export your selected Gmail™ contacts to an Outlook™ CSV file.
    • Open the file, 'Select all' (Ctrl+A), and copy (Ctrl+C).
    • Paste them into the green shaded cell (B1) on the Contacts sheet in AddressBlockApp.
  2. Customization
    • Utilize the 'Name' dropdown box on the Address sheet. Copy and paste it into your desired cell on your spreadsheet.
  3. Formula Integration
    • Copy each cell's formula on the Address sheet and paste them into the formula bar of your chosen cells.
    • Edit the formulas by updating references to the cell where you placed the dropdown box.
  4. Congratulations!
    • Enjoy the benefits of an organized and efficient management experience.
